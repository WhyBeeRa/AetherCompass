# Quick Start by Role

Don't know where to begin? Find your role below. Follow the 3 steps. Get your first real output in under 10 minutes.

## After Your First Output

Once you've created your first output:

1. **Run the loop** - Use prompt `04-close-the-loop.md` to capture what worked
2. **Try another track** - You're not locked to one track. Mix and match.
3. **Explore bonus prompts** - Check `T-tools/01-prompts/04-BONUS/` for advanced features
4. **Build new agents** - Use prompt `04-BONUS/01-create-new-agent.md` to add specialists
