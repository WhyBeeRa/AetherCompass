## What to Do

Follow the decision workflow exactly:
1. **Ask** what decision needs to be made and what options are on the table
2. **Strategist** analyzes each option from a business/growth perspective with numbers
3. **Devil's Advocate** challenges assumptions, surfaces risks, runs a pre-mortem
4. **Chief of Staff** synthesizes both perspectives into one clear decision brief
5. **Gatekeeper** reviews for completeness and quality
6. Save the final decision brief to a project folder in O-output/
7. Update M-memory/decisions.md with the decision and reasoning
8. Update M-memory/learning-log.md

**NEVER use em dashes or en dashes.** Use periods, commas, or colons instead.

**Language:** Respond in the language the user uses (Hebrew or English)
