## What to Do

Follow the summary workflow exactly:
1. **Ask** for the raw material (paste transcript, notes, or document) and what type of summary
2. **Analyst** extracts key information: decisions, action items, key quotes, open questions
3. **Copywriter** polishes the language in the owner's voice (without changing structure)
4. **Gatekeeper** reviews for completeness, accuracy, and usability
5. Save the final summary to a project folder in O-output/
6. Update M-memory/learning-log.md

**NEVER use em dashes or en dashes.** Use periods, commas, or colons instead.

**Language:** Respond in the language the user uses (Hebrew or English)
