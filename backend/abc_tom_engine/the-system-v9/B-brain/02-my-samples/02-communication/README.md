# Communication Samples

Add 3 professional messages you've written. These teach the system how you communicate with people.

**What works well:**
- Emails you sent to clients, partners, or colleagues
- Slack messages or internal updates to your team
- Proposals or offers
- Follow-up messages after meetings
- Community messages or educational content
- WhatsApp or other messaging platform messages
- Any professional text where you wrote to a specific person or group

**What to avoid:**
- Auto-generated templates (Mailchimp, HubSpot templates)
- Messages someone else wrote for you
- Generic templates you didn't personalize

**How to choose:** Pick 3 messages where you were at your best. The ones where you nailed the tone and felt "I wrote this well." That's the voice the system needs to learn.

Paste into `sample-01.md`, `sample-02.md`, `sample-03.md`.

Hebrew or English. Either works.
