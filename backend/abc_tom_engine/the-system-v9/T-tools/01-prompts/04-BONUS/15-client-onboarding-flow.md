# Prompt: Create a Client Onboarding Flow

You just signed a new client. Now what?

This prompt generates a complete onboarding flow in your voice: an intake questionnaire to learn about the client, a welcome email that sets the tone, and a kickoff agenda that makes you look organized. All customized to your business and how you work.

Copy and paste this into your chat (fill in the details first):

---

```
I want to create a client onboarding flow for my business. Here's the context:

---
My service/offering: [What do you sell or deliver? e.g., "brand design packages", "marketing consulting", "workshop facilitation", "coaching sessions"]
Typical client: [Who are your clients? e.g., "startup founders", "marketing managers at mid-size companies", "small business owners"]
What I need from clients before starting: [e.g., "brand guidelines, target audience info, competitors", "access to their systems, team structure", "goals and timeline"]
My tone with new clients: [e.g., "warm but professional", "casual and direct", "structured and thorough"]
---

Adam, please manage this project:

1. Pre-flight check: Read C-core/project-brief.md, C-core/voice-dna.md, and C-core/icp-profile.md. If any of these files are empty or still have placeholder text, STOP and tell me which files need to be filled first.

2. Read my communication samples: B-brain/02-my-samples/02-communication/

3. Read past learnings: M-memory/learning-log.md

4. Create 3 documents, all in MY voice:

   **Document 1: Client Intake Questionnaire**
   - 8-12 questions that get me everything I need to start working
   - Mix of practical questions (timeline, budget, access) and strategic ones (goals, success criteria, past attempts)
   - Written in a way that's easy for the client to fill out (not intimidating)
   - Include a brief intro paragraph explaining why these questions matter

   **Document 2: Welcome Email**
   - The first email a new client gets from me after signing
   - Sets expectations: what happens next, timeline, how we communicate
   - Builds excitement about working together
   - Includes the intake questionnaire as an attachment or inline
   - Ends with a clear next step

   **Document 3: Kickoff Meeting Agenda**
   - A 30-60 minute agenda for the first meeting
   - Reviews their answers from the intake questionnaire
   - Aligns on goals, timeline, and deliverables
   - Covers communication preferences and working style
   - Ends with clear action items for both sides

5. Create a new folder in O-output/ for this project, save all 3 documents there

6. Send to the Gatekeeper for review:
   - Does everything sound like me?
   - Is it professional without being corporate?
   - Would a client feel good receiving this?

7. Update M-memory/learning-log.md

Show me all 3 documents and tell me if they're ready to use.
```

---

## Why This Matters

The first interaction after signing sets the tone for the entire relationship. A smooth onboarding flow:
- Makes you look professional and organized
- Gets you the information you need to do great work
- Reduces back-and-forth in the first weeks
- Builds client confidence from day one

Most people wing it. You won't.
