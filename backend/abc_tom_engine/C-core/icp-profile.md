# Aether - Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## The Core Philosophy: Community First, Business Second
Aether is currently in its community-building phase. Before we monetize through marketing agencies or enterprise clients, we are building a movement. The agents must treat users as co-creators, "Truth Seekers", and peers who share our absolute zero-tolerance policy for AI hype. The ultimate business endgame is B2B, but the current operational focus is 100% community trust and gamified participation.

## Primary Target (Current Phase): The "Truth Seekers" & Early Adopters
- Roles: Tech enthusiasts, AI power-users, developers, cynical creators, and early adopters.
- Core Pain Points: Exhausted by fake reviews, hidden paywalls, and AI influencers pushing tools that fail in production. They feel the AI ecosystem is polluted with marketing garbage.
- Needs: Radical transparency and a platform that respects their intelligence. They want to contribute to a genuine "Source of Truth", participate in Elo Battles to prove which tool is actually better, and earn status (Badges/Authority) within a community of peers.

## Secondary Target (The Endgame): The Budget Holders
- Roles: Digital Marketing Managers, Campaign Managers, CMOs, and IT Directors.
- Core Pain Points: Wasting budget on overhyped tools, Shadow AI risks, and lack of clear ROI.
- Needs: They will eventually consume the data generated by our primary community. They need the "Trust Score" and "Time to Value" metrics (validated by the Truth Seekers) to make safe, data-driven purchasing decisions.

## Psychological Profile
The Aether user is smart, highly skeptical, and instantly recognizes marketing BS. They want raw data, visual proof, and a platform that isn't afraid to brutally downgrade a famous tool if it underperforms. They are here for the mission: cleaning up the AI industry.
