# Aether (The AI Compass) - Project Brief

## Core Identity
Aether is an objective AI tool matching and ranking engine (Intent Engine). It is absolutely not a generic chatbot. The platform acts as the "Verification Layer" for the AI industry, transforming raw marketing claims into verified, laboratory-tested data. 

## The Core Mechanism
Users input a free-text problem or task (Intent). The engine analyzes the semantic need and presents the most accurate, highest-ranked tools for that specific task, eliminating the need to browse categories or search by tool name.

## Business Strategy & Phasing
1. Phase 1: Niche King (Digital Marketing & Creative)
Focusing on the highest-spending sectors (AdTech, Content, Video). The goal is to prove ROI. Aether does not just recommend tools - it saves money and time by highlighting the tools with the best "Time to Value".

2. Phase 2: The Trust Layer (Scale)
Establishing the "Aether Badge" as the industry standard for AI reliability. Providing an API and Enterprise Dashboards to prevent Shadow AI, blocking tools with low Privacy Grades and serving as a cybersecurity and governance asset.

3. Phase 3: Exit Strategy (Proprietary Data)
Building the world's largest behavioral database regarding human interaction with AI models. The true asset is the proprietary data: identifying Model Drift, uncovering fraudulent tools, and predicting market spending trends before financial reports are published.

## Key Metrics (The Aether Standard)
- Trust Score: 1-100 scale based strictly on transparency, lack of hidden paywalls, and raw community sentiment.
- Time to Value: The time required for a professional to extract a high-quality deliverable from the moment of registration.
- Privacy Grade: Enterprise-level security and data usage evaluation.
- Skill Multiplier: How effectively the tool elevates a junior employee's output to a senior level.
- Hallucination Score: The reliability index of the generated output.
